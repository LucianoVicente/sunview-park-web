Star activity

1,350-metre zip line

The jewel of Sunview Park: the longest zip line in Andalusia and the second longest in all of Spain. Fly through the air at over 100 km/h, 190 m high, with the Málaga valley at your feet. An adrenaline rush you'll never forget.

  • 1,350 m course over the valley
  • Up to 100 km/h
  • 190 m maximum altitude
  • Safety equipment and certified instructors

Archery

Focus, a steady hand and bullseye. Our archery range is perfect to challenge your friends or get started in this ancient sport. Suitable for all ages and levels, always supervised by our instructors.

  • Equipment and bows included
  • Specialised instructors
  • Ideal for groups and families
  • Competitive fun guaranteed

Open-top Land Rover ride

Tour the mountain aboard our open-top Land Rover and enjoy the natural surroundings and the views over the Málaga valley from a different perspective. It's the perfect alternative if you'd rather not take on the super zip line — and if you come with a group that jumps the zip line, you'll do the ride together with them.

  • Mountain route with valley views
  • Open-top, open-air vehicle
  • The ideal alternative to the zip line
  • Included in all our packs
Book now

Disc Golf

Golf, but with flying discs and out in nature. Complete the course aiming to finish each hole in the fewest throws. A super fun, relaxed and social activity, perfect to combine with the rest of the park.

  • Course in a natural setting
  • Easy to learn, hard to master
  • Perfect for groups and team building
  • Discs included
Book now

Horse Riding

Connect with nature at the gentle pace of our horses. Ride the trails around Alhaurín de la Torre enjoying the scenery and spectacular views. An experience for everyone, even for those who have never ridden before.

  • Guided trails through the natural surroundings
  • Gentle horses, suitable for beginners
  • Panoramic valley views
  • A different way to experience the park
