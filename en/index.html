Welcome

Adventure, nature and pure adrenaline

Sunview Park Adventure is the largest adventure park in the province of Málaga. Just 20 km from the city, surrounded by nature, with activities for all ages and thrill levels.

  • The longest zip line in Andalusia, 2nd longest in Spain
  • Activities for families, groups and companies
  • Bar and terrace with panoramic views
